This graph is based on the data collected during our first laboratory experience. It therefore represents cosmic ray flux variation as a function of the detector’s inclination measured in radians. The 6 points in the table represent the analysis result of the 6 measurements made at 6 increasing inclinations.

Angle (rad) Rate (Hz)
0 0.00415
0.34 0.003678
0.70 0.002734
1.04 0.00195
1.39 0.001
1.57 0.00126

To find the ordinate of those points, we calculated the mean value of each measurement and reported it on the graph relative to the slope of the corresponding measurement.
The blue curve consists of a quadratic function found by interpolating the points with the help of Python programming language.
